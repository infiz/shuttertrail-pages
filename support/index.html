App support

How can we help?

Find answers to common recording and GPX-export questions, or contact Shutter Trail support.

Recording in the background

For background recording, allow Shutter Trail to access your location Always. Standard mode uses battery-efficient significant location changes while the app is in the background. High Accuracy keeps an active, more precise background recording session and uses more battery.

If the trail stops updating

  • Confirm that Record Trail is enabled in Settings.
  • Open iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Shutter Trail and choose Always.
  • Enable Precise Location if you use High Accuracy.
  • Do not force-quit the app while you expect background recording.
  • Open Recording Health inside Shutter Trail to inspect the active location services.

Exporting to Lightroom Classic

Create a Trip for the desired time range, preview its trail, and export the GPX file. For easier automatic geotagging, use the free Shutter Trail Lightroom plug-in to match selected photos with recorded locations while accounting for each photo’s UTC offset.

Contact support

Email shuttertrail.support@gmail.com and include your iPhone model, iOS version, Shutter Trail version, and a short description of what happened. You can also open a public GitHub issue, but do not post private location data or GPX files there.
