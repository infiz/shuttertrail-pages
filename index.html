For photographers

Record the trail. Tag the photographs.

Shutter Trail records a timestamped GPS trail on your iPhone. After the shoot, define a trip by time range and export a GPX file for Lightroom Classic to match locations with your photographs.

Simple by design

Open the app to begin standard recording. Enable High Accuracy when you need a more consistent active background session, then turn it off when the shoot is over to reduce battery use.
