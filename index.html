For photographers

One location trail for every camera.

Keep your photography locations in one place without relying on camera-to-phone connections. Shutter Trail records where you go so Lightroom Classic can add those locations to photos from one or multiple cameras.

Simple by design

1. Record your shoot. Open Shutter Trail to start recording. Turn High Accuracy on when you want more consistent background coverage, and turn it off when you no longer need it to save battery.

2. Geotag your photos. After the shoot, create a Trip for the time range you need and export its GPX file. Import it into Lightroom Classic to automatically match your photos with locations by capture time.
