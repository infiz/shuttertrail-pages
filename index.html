For photographers

One location trail for every camera.

Keep your photography locations in one place without relying on camera-to-phone connections. Shutter Trail records where you go and provides a Lightroom Classic plug-in that automatically adds those locations to photos from one or multiple cameras.

Simple by design

1. Record your shoot. Open Shutter Trail to start recording. Turn High Accuracy on when you want more consistent background coverage, and turn it off when you no longer need it to save battery.

2. Automatically geotag your photos. After the shoot, create a Trip for the time range you need and export its GPX file. Use the free Shutter Trail Lightroom plug-in to match selected photos with recorded locations by capture time—without manually calculating each camera’s UTC offset.

Lightroom Classic plug-in

Automatically add your recorded locations in Lightroom.

Shutter Trail also provides a free Lightroom Classic plug-in. It matches selected photos with one or more GPX tracks while respecting the UTC offset stored in each photo, making multi-camera geotagging faster and less error-prone.
