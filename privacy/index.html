Privacy Policy

Effective July 19, 2026

Shutter Trail is designed to keep your sensitive location history under your control.

Information Shutter Trail uses

With your permission, Shutter Trail uses precise or approximate location information—including coordinates, timestamps, altitude, speed, course, and accuracy—to display your position, record trails, build trip previews, and create GPX exports.

Background location

If you grant Always location access, Shutter Trail can record while the app is in the background. High Accuracy uses continuous precise background updates and may display the iOS background location indicator. You can disable High Accuracy, stop trail recording, change location permission, or turn off Location Services at any time.

Storage and sharing

Recorded trails, indexes, trip definitions, and app preferences are stored locally on your device. Shutter Trail does not upload your location history to our servers. A GPX file leaves the app only when you choose to export it through the iOS share sheet; the destination you select then handles that file under its own privacy practices.

Notifications

If you enable recording reminders, Shutter Trail schedules local notifications on your device. These reminders do not require your trail history to be sent to us.

Analytics, advertising, and tracking

Shutter Trail does not include advertising, third-party analytics, or cross-app tracking. We do not sell personal information.

Apple services

The app relies on Apple services such as Core Location, MapKit, notifications, the share sheet, and App Store distribution. Apple may process information under Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Data retention and deletion

Your recorded data remains on your device until you remove it using Shutter Trail’s data-cleanup controls or delete the app. Files exported to another app or service must be deleted separately from that destination.

Children’s privacy

Shutter Trail is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app changes. The revised policy will appear here with a new effective date.

Questions

For privacy questions or requests, email shuttertrail.support@gmail.com. You may also visit the Shutter Trail support page. Do not include private location data in a public GitHub issue.
